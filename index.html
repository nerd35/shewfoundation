ABOUT S.H.E.W

S.H.E.W Foundation

SPREADING HOPE EVERYWHERE FOUNDATION (S.H.E.W) is a nonprofit and charity organization founded in November 2018, but it registration was approved may 2019, which exist to give hope to vulnerable people everywhere (especially young people and women) and work with others to build a better world for all.
